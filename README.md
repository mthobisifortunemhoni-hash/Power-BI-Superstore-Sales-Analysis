# Power-BI-Superstore-Sales-Analysis

# Objectives
  Analyze sales performance using Power BI and identify business insights from the Superstore dataset.

# Visualization
![Sales by Region](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/mthobisifortunemhoni-hash/Power-BI-Superstore-Sales-Analysis/main/Sales-by-region.png)

# Findings
 West recorded the highest sales.
 East recorded the second-highest sales.
 Central ranked third.
 South recorded the lowest sales.

# Business Insight
 The West region is currently the strongest-performing region in terms of sales.
 The South region appears to be underperforming compared with the other regions and should be investigated further.

# Recommendations
 The business should investigate questions such as:
 Why is the West performing so well?
 Are there more customers in the West?
 Does the West sell higher-value products?
 Does the South have fewer customers?
 Are there supply chain or marketing issues affecting the South?
